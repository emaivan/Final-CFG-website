What is CÉLIA?

o.2.

We are professional interior designers specialising in minimalist designs. We help those wanting to embrace the future to find peace, cosiness and creativity in simplicity. Minimalist interior design does not have to be bland, but, with the right colors and furniture pieces, it can transform the home to reflect the uniqueness of its owners themselves. A cluttered home does not equal a better home and we are here to prove it.

Our specialists have spent numerous years studying the psychology of interior designing and have transferred their skills to provide the best service possible to our clients. Thus, as a company, we thrive on close communication with our clients and provide them with the opportunity to be part of every single step of the creative process. After all, it is you your home needs to reflect.

We also make custom furniture for those wanting to make a statement. Each piece is carefully hand-crafted from recycled and reclaimed materials. It is our priority to leave as small an ecological footprint as possible. Not to worry, your furniture will be of top quality.
